Forged for builders,
not just players.

Cogwheel was born in a Create mod server room — where lag killed more contraptions than creepers ever did. We set out to build hosting that treats Minecraft like the serious engineering platform it is: precise, reliable, and ridiculously fast.

Every node we deploy is tuned for modpacks first. That means Aikar's flags by default, NVMe for chunk I/O, and support staff who actually know what a Schematicannon is.

Mission

Democratize high-performance Minecraft hosting. No compromises on TPS, no guesswork on modpacks, and no ticket queues longer than a redstone tick.

Services

Shared cloud nodes, bare-metal rigs, and managed modpack deployment. From vanilla friend groups to 500-player modded networks.

Philosophy

We measure success in stress units, not sales calls. If your flywheel stalls, we consider it a personal failure.
